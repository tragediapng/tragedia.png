About me

I'm Dia, a tattoo artist based in Brno, creating abstract, organic and playful tattoos. I love turning random ideas, strange shapes and everyday details into something permanent, creating pieces that feel personal rather than perfect.

Alongside custom work, I also create original flashes and freehand designs. Whether you already have a clear vision or just a rough idea, we'll shape it into something that's completely yours.
